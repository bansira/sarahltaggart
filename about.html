Sarah's short stories have appeared in The Malahat Review and The Fiddlehead. She was longlisted for the Journey Prize, shortlisted for The Fiddlehead's annual contest and the Community Arts Council of Victoria's literary competition, and won the Malahat Review's Jack Hodgins Founders' Award for Fiction and the BC Federation of Writers Literary Writes prize. She was also a recipient of a British Columbia Arts Council Senior Scholarship Award. She holds an MFA from the University of Saskatchewan, where she earned the New Faculty Graduate Student Award and was nominated for a Graduate Student Thesis Award. Sarah lives in Toronto.
